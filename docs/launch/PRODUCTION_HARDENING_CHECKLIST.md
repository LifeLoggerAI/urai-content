# URAI Content Production Hardening Checklist

Domain: uraicontent.com

Before production approval:

- Confirm all public routes exist.
- Confirm every public page has metadata.
- Confirm every public page links to URAI Privacy.
- Confirm Motion, Cinema, Music, Visuals, Drops, Series, and Licensing routes are claim-safe.
- Confirm app traffic routes to https://urai.app/start.
- Confirm commercial production traffic routes to https://uraistudio.com/start.
- Confirm Storytime traffic routes to https://uraistorytime.com when available.
- Confirm licensing/contact forms preserve UTM/source fields.
- Confirm forms write to approved backend.
- Confirm no unpublished/internal media is public.
- Confirm no guaranteed virality, revenue, follower growth, or platform monetization claims.
- Run build, typecheck, tests, and QA.
- Record DNS, SSL, deploy URL, latest commit, and owner approval.
