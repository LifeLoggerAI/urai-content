## Planning mode

This is the release-state planning calendar. Internally, each scheduled drop is planned as live by its release day. Public claims remain receipt-gated: do not publish a live claim until source/deployment proof, screenshots/video, legal/privacy/security approval, accessibility review where required, and exact live authority exist.

## Campaign line

For 45 days, UrAi opens one door every day.
On Day 45, you meet the person who built it.
For the next 45 days, you see why the world needed it.

## Cadence

- Every day: one real reveal.
- Every 2 days: a feature bomb.
- Every 3 days: a category or press thesis.
- Every 7 days: a weekly shockwave.
- Day 45: founder reveal.
- Day 90: the door after the door.

## Production fields for every day

Each daily drop needs:

1. Hero hook
2. 15-30 second short video
3. Still image or screenshot
4. 19+ language caption pack
5. English long-form post
6. Press angle
7. Proof artifact or disclosure boundary
8. Claim level: Live, Preview, Roadmap, Research, or Internal
9. Owner
10. Go/no-go gate

## Language pack

Default launch language set: English, Mandarin Simplified, Hindi, Spanish, French, Standard Arabic, Bengali, Portuguese Brazilian, Russian, Urdu, Indonesian, German, Japanese, Swahili, Turkish, Vietnamese, Tagalog/Filipino, Korean, Italian, Persian/Farsi.

## Claim guardrails

Do not claim guaranteed medical outcomes, diagnosis, therapy, guaranteed rescue, guaranteed earnings, guaranteed user numbers, or unreviewed legal/security/privacy statements.

Preferred wording: support, reflection, pattern, possible signal, preview, opt-in, anonymized, consented, governed, receipt-backed, user-owned.

## Compounding thesis

Days 1-45 make the world undeniable.
Days 46-90 make the mission undeniable.
After Day 90, users become the engine.

Every user can add memories, corrections, artifacts, language signal, shareable Storytime outputs, and opt-in anonymized patterns. That creates product compounding, social compounding, trust compounding, media compounding, language compounding, and RuAI/B2B compounding.

## Internal scale scenarios, not public promises

- 3 months: signal. UrAi should be known as the spatial life-memory AI system. Strong MRR path: $40k-$225k. Nuclear path: $1M+ MRR.
- 6 months: proof. Retention, onboarding, Replay/Storytime, and one B2B/RuAI loop decide whether hype becomes company. Strong path: $250k-$1.2M MRR. Nuclear path: $7M MRR.
- 12 months: leverage. UrAi becomes a platform thesis if consumer retention, creator artifacts, RuAI pilots, privacy trust, and multilingual growth hold. Strong path: $1M-$7.5M MRR. Nuclear path: $25M+ MRR.
