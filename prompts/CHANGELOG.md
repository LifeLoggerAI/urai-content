## Change policy

- Use semantic-style prompt versions: major for behavioral contract changes, minor for backward-compatible capability additions, and patch for clarifications or typo fixes.
- Describe the expected behavioral impact of every material prompt change.
- Keep the Google Doc and repository copy synchronized.
- Preserve rollback history through Git commits rather than silently replacing prior behavior.
