Adaptable templates
The three templates below are deliberately narrower than the master prompt. The quick-scan version optimizes for orientation and speed. The deep literature review most closely tracks PRISMA, Cochrane, and GRADE-style rigor. The market and competitive analysis version shifts the source hierarchy toward first-party product materials, filings, patents, pricing pages, and regulator sources. 


Template        Best for        Source emphasis        Method style        Typical output        Indicative effort
Quick scan        Rapid orientation, current-state scan, shortlist questions        Official docs, a few strong primary sources, selective secondary context        Broad-to-focused scan, minimal but explicit screening        800–1,800 words + 1–3 tables        2–5 hours
Deep literature review        Scientific, medical, policy, or technical evidence where completeness matters        Original papers, systematic reviews, registries, standards, regulatory sources        PRISMA-like search log, evidence matrix, confidence grading        3,000–8,000 words + evidence tables + flow summary        16–46 hours
Market and competitive analysis        Vendor, product, pricing, IP, regulatory, GTM, positioning        Company first-party docs, filings, patents, regulator sources, limited secondary triangulation        Competitive mapping, feature/pricing comparison, moat analysis        2,000–5,000 words + comparison tables + timeline        10–28 hours
